Quick Start

Everyday actions

No account is required. Tasks, preferences, and object matching stay on your device.

01

Create a task

Enter a title, choose a priority, and tap “+”. Thingdo matches the title to a 3D object on your device.

02

Switch tasks

Drag the 3D ring or task strip to move between tasks, or switch to list mode to view and manage everything.

03

Complete or delete

Swipe up on the 3D stage to toggle completion. Use a longer downward swipe to delete the current task.

04

Sound and haptics

Open Settings to turn Sound or Haptics off independently.

Troubleshooting

Frequently asked questions

Does Thingdo need an account or internet connection?

No account is required. Tasks, settings, and object matching are handled on your device, so the app works offline. Version 1.0 does not include cloud sync.

Why does Thingdo request camera access?

Camera access is optional and only powers the camera-object preview and instant-photo effect. Other task features remain available without it.

What if the matched object is not accurate?

Try a shorter, more specific task title. If matching fails, Thingdo shows a default cube while task creation, completion, deletion, and filtering continue to work.

How do I remove local data?

You can delete tasks individually in the app. To remove all local data, choose Delete App in iOS; Offload App preserves documents and data. Apple device backups may retain a copy.

常用操作

Thingdo 不需要账号，任务创建、物体匹配与设置均在设备本地完成。

01

创建任务

在顶部输入任务标题，选择优先级后点击“+”。App 会在设备上自动匹配一个 3D 物体。

问题排查

常见问题

App 需要账号或网络吗？

不需要账号。任务、设置和物体匹配都在设备本地完成，断网也可以使用。当前版本不提供云同步，数据不会自动同步到其他设备。

为什么会请求相机权限？

相机权限只用于“相机物体”的实时预览和 App 内拍立得互动。拒绝权限后会显示静态示例画面，不影响普通待办功能。

物体匹配不准确怎么办？

请尝试使用更明确、简短的任务标题。匹配失败时会显示默认方块，但任务的创建、完成、删除和筛选仍可正常使用。

如何删除本地数据？

你可以在 App 内逐条删除任务。如需移除全部本地数据，请在 iOS 中选择“删除 App”，不要选择“卸载 App”；“卸载 App”会保留文稿与数据。Apple 设备备份中可能仍保留由你或 Apple 管理的副本。
