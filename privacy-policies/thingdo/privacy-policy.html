English 简体中文
At a glance: Thingdo is designed to work locally on your device. We do not operate a server that receives your tasks, settings, or camera images. The App does not use accounts, advertising, tracking, analytics, cloud sync, push notifications, or third-party crash-reporting services. Camera access is optional and is used only for an in-app live preview and temporary visual effect.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy explains how Haichao Zhang ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the Thingdo iOS application (the "App"), contact us for support, or visit this Privacy Policy page.

This Policy describes the version of the App identified above. If we later introduce features such as online accounts, cloud sync, remote analytics, subscriptions, or notifications, we will update this Policy before those features begin processing information.

2. Information processed locally on your device

2.1 Tasks and settings

The App stores the following information in its local app sandbox:

  • task information, including a task identifier, free-form task title, priority, completion status, creation and completion times, and the visual object assigned to the task; and
  • sound and haptic-feedback preferences.

Task titles are classified by an on-device model so the App can select a visual object. This processing happens locally. We do not receive the title or the model result.

Because task titles are free-form, please avoid entering sensitive information that you do not want stored on your device.

2.2 Device and Apple backups

The App does not transmit your task or settings data to us or to our servers. Depending on your device and backup settings, however, the App's local data may be included in a device, Finder/iTunes, or iCloud backup managed by you or Apple. We cannot access or control those backups. Apple's handling of backup data is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

3. Camera access

With your permission, the App accesses the front camera when you activate the camera-object feature. Camera frames are used for an in-app live preview and a temporary instant-photo visual effect. Frames are processed in memory, are not saved to your Photos library or the App's files, and are not transmitted to us or any third party by the App. Related in-memory resources are released when they are no longer needed or when the relevant App resources are discarded.

Camera access is optional. If you deny or later revoke permission, the rest of the App remains available and the camera-object feature uses placeholder imagery instead. You can manage camera permission in iOS Settings.

4. Information the App does not request or transmit to us

The App does not request or automatically transmit to us:

  • names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials;
  • precise or approximate location;
  • contacts, calendars, health data, or microphone recordings;
  • photos or videos from your library;
  • payment or purchase information;
  • advertising identifiers or other identifiers for tracking;
  • product-interaction analytics or behavioral profiles; or
  • crash or performance data through a third-party SDK.

If you choose to include any of this information in a free-form task title, it remains stored locally as described in Section 2 and is not transmitted to us by the App.

We do not use the App to sell personal information, share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, serve targeted advertising, or track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

5. Information you choose to send us

If you email us, we receive the information you choose to provide, which may include your email address, display name, message, attachments, and basic email metadata such as the date and subject. We use this information only to respond to your request, investigate the issue you report, maintain and improve the App's reliability, protect the App and our users, or comply with law. Please do not send sensitive personal information that is unrelated to your request.

Our support address is provided through Microsoft Outlook. Microsoft may process email data on our behalf or as an independent service provider under the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

6. App Store, system services, and this policy page

Apple may independently process App Store activity, purchases, device backups, and diagnostics according to your Apple settings and the Apple Privacy Policy. If Apple makes aggregated App Store information or user-authorized diagnostic reports available to us, we use them only to understand App distribution and maintain reliability. These services do not give us access to your task content or camera frames.

This Privacy Policy page is hosted by GitHub. We do not add analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts to this page. GitHub may process technical information such as IP address, browser information, and access logs under the GitHub General Privacy Statement.

7. Purposes and legal bases

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, the basis depends on the jurisdiction and the context:

  • your system permission enables the optional camera preview on your device; we do not receive the camera data;
  • support communications are processed as necessary to respond to your request, provide the service, or take steps you ask us to take;
  • where recognized by applicable law, we rely on our legitimate interests in securing, maintaining, and improving the App based on issues or diagnostics reported to us, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights; and
  • as necessary to comply with applicable legal obligations.

For users in China, we process support communications based on consent where consent is required, as necessary to respond to or perform a service requested by you where permitted by law, or to fulfill legal duties. For users in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, the applicable bases may include performing our service or taking requested steps, legitimate interests as described above, legal obligations, and consent where we specifically request it. You may withdraw consent at any time, without affecting processing already lawfully completed.

The on-device task classification only selects a visual object. It is not used to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you.

8. Disclosure of information

We do not sell or rent personal information. Because the App does not transmit your tasks, settings, or camera frames to us, we do not disclose that information to third parties.

Information you voluntarily send by email may be processed by our email provider as described above. We may also disclose information if required by law, legal process, or a valid governmental request, or when reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, and security.

9. International processing

The App does not send your locally stored task data or camera frames to us across borders. Support messages are received and handled by the operator in China. Microsoft provides the email infrastructure and may store or process the email address, message, attachments, and email metadata in locations where it operates. If you visit this page, GitHub may process associated technical data in locations where it operates. Their contact information, purposes, processing practices, and available rights are described in the privacy statements linked in Sections 5 and 6. Where applicable law requires consent or another safeguard for cross-border processing, we will apply the required measure.

10. Retention and deletion

  • Tasks: retained locally until you delete an individual task or delete the App.
  • Settings: retained locally until you change them or delete the App.
  • Camera frames: processed only in memory for the live preview and temporary visual effect. Related in-memory resources are released when they are no longer needed or when the relevant App resources are discarded.
  • Support email: retained only for the shortest period reasonably necessary to respond to the request, maintain security, resolve a dispute, or comply with law, and then deleted or irreversibly anonymized. Backup copies may remain until they are overwritten in the ordinary course by the email provider.

Deleting the App removes its active local sandbox from that device. Copies may remain in device or iCloud backups controlled by you or Apple until those backups are deleted or expire under the applicable backup settings. You can manage backups through your device or Apple account.

11. Your choices and privacy rights

  • You can delete tasks individually within the App.
  • You can delete the App to remove its active local data from the device, subject to backups described above.
  • You can grant or revoke camera access in iOS Settings.
  • There is no Thingdo account or server-side task copy to delete or export.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to know about, access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to our processing of personal information; obtain a portable copy; withdraw consent; and lodge a complaint with a competent privacy or data-protection authority. These rights mainly apply to information you voluntarily send us, because we cannot access information that remains only on your device. To exercise a right, email us at shenshuoyouguang@outlook.com. We normally verify a request through the email address that originally contacted us, and will avoid requesting additional information unless it is reasonably necessary. We will respond without undue delay and within the period required by applicable law. If we cannot fulfill a request, we will explain the reason and the available review or complaint channel, unless law prevents us from doing so.

Users in China may also request an explanation of our processing rules and exercise applicable rights to access and copy, correct or supplement, delete, restrict or refuse processing, and withdraw consent. Users in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom may object to processing based on legitimate interests and may complain to their local data-protection authority.

For California residents, we do not sell personal information, share it for cross-context behavioral advertising, or use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes that require a right to limit. We will not discriminate against you for exercising an applicable privacy right.

12. Children's privacy

Thingdo is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information through the App from children, including children under 14 in China, under 13 in the United States, or under the applicable minimum age in another jurisdiction. If you believe a child has sent personal information to our support email, please contact us so we can delete it.

13. Security

We minimize risk by keeping App data within the iOS app sandbox and by not operating a server that receives your tasks or camera frames. No method of storage or communication is completely secure, and the security of device backups and email also depends on the security settings and practices of you and the relevant service providers.

14. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy when the App's features, data practices, or legal requirements change. We will post the updated version at this URL and revise the effective or last-updated date. If a change materially affects how information is processed, we will provide any additional notice or consent required by applicable law before the new practice begins.

二、仅在您的设备上处理的信息

2.1 任务与设置

本应用会在应用本地沙盒中保存以下信息：

  • 任务信息，包括任务标识符、自由输入的任务标题、优先级、完成状态、创建和完成时间，以及为任务匹配的视觉物体；
  • 声音和触感反馈偏好。

本应用使用设备内置模型对任务标题进行分类，以选择对应的视觉物体。该处理完全在本地完成，我们不会收到任务标题或模型分类结果。

任务标题可自由输入，请不要输入您不希望保存在设备上的敏感信息。

本应用不会将您的任务或设置数据传输给我们或我们的服务器。但是，根据您的设备和备份设置，本应用的本地数据可能被纳入由您或 Apple 管理的设备备份、Finder/iTunes 备份或 iCloud 备份。我们无法访问或控制这些备份。Apple 对备份数据的处理适用其条款与隐私政策。

三、相机权限

经您授权，当您启用“相机物体”功能时，本应用会访问前置摄像头。相机画面仅用于应用内实时预览及临时拍立得视觉效果。画面只在内存中处理，不会保存到系统相册或本应用文件，也不会由本应用传输给我们或任何第三方。相关内存资源会在不再需要时或相关应用资源被释放时清除。

相机权限为可选权限。拒绝或撤回授权不会影响本应用的其他功能，“相机物体”将改用示例画面。您可以在 iOS“设置”中管理相机权限。
